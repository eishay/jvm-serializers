jvm-serializers by eishay

Benchmark comparing serialization libraries on the JVM

The project is about comparing serialization performance and other aspects of serialization libraries on the JVM. Overall we have benchmarks for protobuf, thrift, java, scala, few implementations of stax, binaryxml, json, xstream, javolution, hessian, avro, sbinary and more. Discussion list at http://groups.google.com/group/java-serialization-benchmarking
